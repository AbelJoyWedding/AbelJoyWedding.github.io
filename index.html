Not Just an
Ordinary Love Story

In Daejeon’s gentle embrace, two paths converged,
One from Seoul’s bustling rhythm, the other a mission emerged.
In a humble restaurant, beneath the evening glow,
Destiny wove threads neither could know.

Her laughter, like sunlight, lit the room so bright,
Her caring heart, a beacon in the quiet night.
He couldn’t look away, his heart struck anew,
A spark ignited, a dream breaking through.

Cafés became their haven, where worlds intertwined,
Language and love blossomed, hearts combined.
She taught him her words; he shared his delight,
Each moment together felt perfectly right.

But the tides of life pulled him back to the West,
Leaving behind the one who had captured his quest.
Yet fortune favored, as fate often dares—
She was bound for the States, with dreams to share.

To New York she came, her spirit bold,
While he forged a new path in the tech world’s fold.
Reunited at last, in that city of dreams,
Their love grew stronger, like rushing streams.

Now they stand, hand in hand, ready to begin,
A life together, where love always wins.
Through every journey, every twist, every turn,
Their hearts remain aflame, forever to burn.

“If we love one another, God abides in us and His love is perfected in us.”

1 John 4:12
